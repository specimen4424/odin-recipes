Grass

Description

Nothing wrong with touching grass every now and then. Go outside enjoy the fresh air and some soft greenery. The best thing about this recipe is that there is no mess, cleanup, or even prep. Just you, yourself, and some nature

Instructions

  1. Check the forecast to observe if it is your preferred weather today
  2. Choose the appropriate attire to go outside.
  3. Proceed out of your abode to the nearest patch of grass.
  4. Take your favorite limb and use it to touch the grass.
  5. Enjoy! You got outside! I promise it's good for you.
