Pad Thai

Description

A popular Thai street food dish typically consisting of fried rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, lime, bean sprouts, and egg, pad Thai has become a staple dish at many Thai restaurants across the United States.

Fun fact, the Thai government has utilized its popularity as a way of spreading Thai culture by supporting the establishment of Thai restaurants across the world beginning in the early 2000s.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Your choice of protein: shrimp, chicken, beef, tofu, etc
  • Bean sprouts
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green onion
  • Peanuts
  • Sauce ingredients
    • Fish sauce
    • Dark soy sauce
    • Light brown sugar
    • Rice vinegar
    • Sriracha hot sauce
    • Creamy peanut butter
    • Optional: tamarind sauce

Instructions

  1. Presoak the rice noodles in boiling water for 20 to 30 minutes and cook according to packet instructions
  2. Combine the ingredients for the sauce and beat the eggs before putitng them to the side
  3. Stir fry your protein, garlic, green onion, and bell peppers
  4. Add the eggs and scramble them until cooked
  5. Finally add the cooked rice noodles, sauce, bean sprouts, and peanuts, and combine all components.
  6. Garnish with additional green onion, lime juice, and peanuts
